Privacy Policy

Simple and transparent — here's what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

Last updated: January 2026
01

Who We Are

Digixta is a digital agency based in Karachi, Pakistan. This policy covers our website at digixta.digital and any communication you have with us.

02

What We Collect

Only what you give us directly — nothing more.

  • Your name, email, and WhatsApp number from the contact form
  • Project details you share with us
  • Basic, anonymous site visit data

We do not collect payment details. Payments go through JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or bank transfer directly.

03

How We Use It

  • To respond to your enquiry or quote request
  • To deliver your project and send updates
  • To improve how our website works

We will never send unsolicited emails or add you to a newsletter without your permission.

04

Data Sharing

We don't sell, rent, or trade your information. The only exceptions are coordinating with a hosting provider on your behalf, or if required by Pakistani law — and only the minimum necessary is shared.

05

Cookies

We use essential cookies (for the site to work) and basic anonymous analytics. No advertising cookies, no cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser anytime.

06

Your Rights

You can ask us what data we hold, correct it, or have it deleted — anytime, no hassle. Just send us a message and we'll sort it within a few business days.

07

Third-Party Links

Links to external sites (portfolio projects, social media) are outside our control. We recommend checking their own privacy policies.
